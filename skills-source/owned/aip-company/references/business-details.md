## Business Model

### Done For You (DFY) - Core Offering

**Current Revenue Model:**
*   **Pricing:** Flat-rate subscriptions, typically $2,499–$3,000 per month.
*   **What's Included:** Four fully managed episodes per month (up to six hours of input).
*   **Add-Ons:** Additional input hours at $500/hour.
*   **Services Covered:** Editing, show notes, thumbnail/short-form content creation, publishing, distribution, and strategic growth guidance.

**Why This Works:**
*   High client retention (sticky service).
*   Scalable service delivery through AI + optimized client operations.
*   Predictable revenue stream.

### Tooling Layer (DIY) - Future Expansion

*   **Strategy:** Convert internal DFY tools into self-serve SaaS products.
*   **Pricing:** Use-case specific pricing models.
*   **Target:** Entry point for smaller creators who may scale into DFY services later.
