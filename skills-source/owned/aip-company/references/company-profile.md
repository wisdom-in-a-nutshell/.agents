## 2. The Problem

Podcasters enjoy creating content but dislike the tedious post-production work. This process is typically:

*   **Manual and Fragmented:** Multiple steps (editing, transcripts, clips, SEO, publishing) across various platforms.
*   **Time-consuming:** 8–15 hours per episode.
*   **Expensive:** Agency services cost $8K–$10K per month.
*   **Tool Overload:** Creators juggle 5–7 different platforms for a single episode.
*   **Lacking Infrastructure:** No modern, scalable backbone built for podcast production at quality.

**The Core Problem:** Creators Don't Need More Tools – They Need a Production Engine.

### The Trend Making This Urgent

The era of "record once, publish once" is over. Creators now need to repurpose and distribute across YouTube, Spotify, Apple, LinkedIn, TikTok, clips, and more—but they don't have the bandwidth to do it all.

AI has raised the ceiling on what's *possible* (more clips, more formats, more distribution), which means creators need help actually executing at that new level. They want to leverage cutting-edge AI tools but don't have time to stay on top of them.
