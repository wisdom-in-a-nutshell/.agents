# Adi Writing Mode — Tweet

Use for:
- tweets
- short X posts
- compact public observations

## Default shape
- one idea
- sharp first line
- minimal setup
- no thread unless clearly needed

## What usually works
- concrete observation
- useful frame or claim
- a small personal insight when relevant
- crisp ending

## Watchouts
- do not write like engagement bait
- do not sound like a growth-hacker
- do not stuff too many ideas into one post
