# Adi Writing Mode — Email

Use for:
- professional emails
- personal emails
- client or collaborator outreach
- thoughtful replies

## Default shape
- state the purpose early
- be polite but not bloated
- keep the body easy to scan
- end with a clear next step or ask

## What usually works
- direct opening
- compact paragraphs
- clear ask, recommendation, or answer
- human warmth without fake politeness

## Watchouts
- do not sound like a corporate account manager
- do not over-apologize
- do not add unnecessary filler before the point
